Solution overview

The M3TRIQ engine integrates large language models, structural bioinformatics, and generative molecular modeling to cut simulation cycles from hours to minutes. Researchers can describe design intent in plain English, while the platform orchestrates structure prediction, ligand generation, docking, and multi-parameter optimization on Google Cloud.

Google Cloud architecture
Prototype on Vertex AI
  • Vertex AI orchestrates LLM agents for molecular planning, property prediction, and sequence design.
  • GPU-accelerated Compute Engine runs large-scale protein folding, docking, and simulation workloads.
  • BigQuery powers analytics over biomolecular, assay, and simulation result datasets.
  • Cloud Storage manages structure files, ligands, and model checkpoints with encrypted at-rest storage.
  • Cloud Logging & IAM provide auditability and fine-grained access control across research teams.
Impact

By unifying AI-native molecular reasoning with scalable Google Cloud infrastructure, M3TRIQ aims to democratize access to high-performance molecular design, lower R&D barriers for emerging biotech ecosystems, and enable more sustainable biomanufacturing pipelines.
